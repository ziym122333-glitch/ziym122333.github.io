Best Creatine Supplements

< img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1599058917765-a780eda07a3e">

Creatine Monohydrate

Creatine monohydrate is the most studied supplement in sports nutrition. It helps increase strength, muscle mass, and training performance. Many athletes use creatine to improve power and recovery.

< img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1579758629938-03607ccdbaba">

Creatine HCL

Creatine HCL is known for better solubility and digestion. Some athletes prefer it because it may cause less bloating. It is a popular alternative to traditional creatine powder.

< img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1554284126-aa88f22d8b74">

Creatine Loading Guide

The creatine loading phase is used to quickly saturate muscles with creatine. A typical protocol is 20g per day for 5-7 days. After loading, users switch to a maintenance dose.
